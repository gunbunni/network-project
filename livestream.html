tokkette2 is a nonstop online livestream that shows a looping video of an empty room. The footage looks like CCTV—there’s no sound, no story, just the same scene playing over and over, 24/7. As time passes, the video slowly breaks down through color shifts, small glitches, and compression artifacts. Influenced by forgotten webcams and passive internet spaces, it’s designed to exist quietly and persistently without interaction.
